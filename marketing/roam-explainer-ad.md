# roam. — "See How It Works" explainer & ad pack

Reusable creative for **Facebook / Instagram (Reels + Feed) / TikTok**, matched
1:1 to the in-app explainer (`client/src/components/how-it-works.tsx`). Record
the explainer running on a phone (or screen-capture the 9:16 column) and you have
the hero cut. All copy, hooks and captions below are ready to paste.

**roam — match on where you've been, not how you look.** Adventure-matching for
Aotearoa: post your real photos, match on shared adventures, discover who you
*almost met*, message free, crew up, and meet on the trail — not in a dead DM.

**Brand:** forest `#0e1a0d` · electric lime `#c8e64a` · cream `#f2ede3` · serif
Playfair Display · mono labels (UPPERCASE, wide tracking). Tone: warm, outdoorsy,
a little cheeky, anti-superficial-dating-app.

## Cut 1 — 9:16 vertical, ~25s (TikTok / Reels / Shorts) ← primary

```
0.0–2.5s  Beat 1  — hook. Hard cut on "face." (most scroll-stop here)
2.5–6.5s  Beat 2  — "adventures" lands in electric lime
6.5–11s   Beat 3  — ALMOST MET 👻 (the differentiator — let it breathe)
11–15s    Beat 4  — free to message
15–19.5s  Beat 5  — crew up / meet IRL (cut to a real hike/surf shot if available)
19.5–25s  Beat 6 + CTA card: logo, "Start your adventure", founding-spots line
```
- **Text-on-screen = the big serif line each beat.** Keep captions burned in
  (most watch muted).
- **Sound:** upbeat indie-folk / lo-fi adventure track, ~120 BPM; punch the beat
  changes to the downbeat.
- **End frame (hold 3s):** `roam.` wordmark + "letsroam.life" + "Match on where
  you've been."

## CTA & offer
- Primary CTA: **Start your adventure** → `letsroam.life/signup`
- Urgency: **"Only N of 50 founding spots left — free Adventurer for life"**
  (pull the live number from `/api/founding-status`; keep it honest).
- Secondary CTA (retargeting): **"See what's on near you"** → events.

## Targeting starters (Meta/TikTok)
- Geo: New Zealand (start Auckland + Queenstown/Wānaka).
- Interests: hiking, tramping, surfing, climbing, Meetup, DOC, camping, travel,
  Strava; 22–40; exclude existing app users.
- Best-performing angle to scale first: **Almost Met** (beat 3) — it's the thing
  no other app has.

## Production notes
- The in-app explainer is the cheapest hero asset: open `letsroam.life`, tap
  "See how it works," screen-record the 9:16 column top-to-bottom. It already
  auto-advances on the timings above.
- Swap in real UGC adventure b-roll where possible — authenticity is the brand
  (roam has a strict **no fake profiles** rule; keep the ads equally real).
- Keep the lime accent for the punch words only; never on full sentences.
