09:15Reconstructing ROAM's interface with streamlined navigation and sticky controlsReconstructing ROAM's interface with streamlined navigation and sticky controlsHere's everything that changed and why it feels different:
Navigation — the top tab bar is completely gone. One navigation system now, four items at the bottom: Discover 🧭, Matches ⚡, the electric ＋ post button floating above the nav line, and Profile 👤. Places/Bucket List lives inside Discover as a scroll section — it doesn't need its own tab at this stage. The whole screen just opened up.
Hero image — up from 340px to 500px. On a standard phone that's roughly 60% of your visible screen before anything else. Photography is the product. Give it the real estate it deserves. The gradient is also deeper now — name and tagline are always readable against any photo without needing a separate background.
Sticky action bar — Pass, Roam Together, and Super Roam (⭐ save for later) are now pinned to the base of the card. No more scrolling to find them. They're always there. On mobile you also get swipe gestures — left to pass, right to roam, with visual overlays that show "PASS" in ember and "ROAM ✦" in electric green as you drag. Subtle arrow hints appear on the very first card to teach the gesture.
Almost Met — now lives only in one place: a small tap-expandable chip on the hero image. Tap it and it opens to the full detail. Tap again to close. No duplicate panel in the card body.
DNA tag row — removed. The overlap bar carries that message on its own. Cleaner.
Match celebration — tap Roam Together and you get a full-screen moment: the two hero photos side by side with a ✦ between them, the shared tags listed, the Almost Met callout if relevant, and a single CTA "Start the adventure →". That emotional peak now actually exists.
Stories — sorted unseen-first by overlap score, so the most relevant unseen story always appears first rather than just chronologically.
Colour discipline — ember orange is now strictly reserved for: the video 🎬 icon, the Almost Met chip border, the video CTA, and warning states. Electric green owns everything positive. Sky blue is informational only.
